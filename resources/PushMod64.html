PushMod64

This tweak was made by me as a successor to the original PushMod, which stopped working as of iOS 11...

PushMod64 gives you additional email fetch options of 3, 5 & 10 minutes, on top of the stock options of 15, 30 & 60 minutes.

Surprisingly, this tweak does not use any noticeable amount of battery on my iPhone X fetching 3 email accounts every 3 minutes. I use this tweak to essentially convert IMAP/POP mailboxes that don't support push. With this tweak set to 3 minutes, for all (most) intents and purposes that is what happens.

 

If you are on iOS 10 or under, please install the original PushMod tweak from ModMyi, as it still works beautifully on iOS 10.

 

Please Note:

PushMod64 makes a backup of the stock settings to restore upon uninstallation of the tweak.

Be careful using any tool that may remove a file appended with .bak, because it may delete your original fetch settings and they won't be able to be restored.
