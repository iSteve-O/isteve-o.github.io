STOCKER13

 

This theme was made by me to help ease my obsessive nature...

Simple & mostly stock looking -that's Stocker13.

Some production icons are ugly, don't fit the color pallet, or just don't sit well within the general symmetry of the iOS home screen.

I aim to fix this with Stocker13!

Please Note:

It may be necessary in order to properly theme the clock icon, or if you do not wish to use a certain icon from this, or any theme, or if you would like to use any other app's icon for a specific app, put the home screen in edit (wiggle) mode & double-tap the icon you wish to change. Then pick whatever icon you'd like to use for that app from the Snowboard alternate icon menu (swipe pages to use icons from other themes or apps you have installed, or to use images from your camera roll).
